In my childhood, my grandmother often made delicious wontons (dumplings) for me during my stay at her house. Her wontons were unlike any wontons in the restaurants. However, my grandmother and I lived far apart, so Grandma's wontons were therefore a rare delicacy for me.

After knowing that I love eating Grandma's wontons, my mom, who seldom cooked because of her busy work, decided to learn to make Grandma's wontons. She even managed to come up with a tastier recipe.

Everytime my mom made wontons for me, she would watch me finishing a whole bowl of them and smile at my praises.

Now in college, I am not able to go home often. Sometimes, I get homesick. I miss my family... And the taste of warmth and love my mom wraps in her wontons. Wait, why don't I try to make wontons myself?
